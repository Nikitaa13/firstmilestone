Explore Krakow

Krakow, a picturesque city in Poland, is known for its history, culture, and stunning architecture. As a visitor, you may find that taking a city tour is an excellent way to discover and explore this beautiful city. There are various types of city tours in Krakow to suit different interests and preferences, whether you’re a history buff, a foodie or an art enthusiast. One popular and efficient way to experience Krakow’s charm is through a City Tour by Electric Golf Cart. These small electric vehicles offer a comfortable and environmentally friendly mode of sightseeing in a city with narrow streets and charming squares. Alternatively, you can opt for a Food Walking Tour to indulge in the vibrant and diverse culinary scene that attracts food enthusiasts from around the world. During your visit to Krakow, you shouldn’t miss the opportunity to explore some of the city’s most fascinating historic sites. The city boasts rich history and cultural heritage, making it a haven for history enthusiasts. Your tour through Krakow’s historic sites should start with a visit to the Old Town. As you walk along the cobblestone streets, you’ll encounter architectural marvels, beautiful squares, and amazing sculptures. Make sure to take a tour around the Main Market Square, St. Mary’s Basilica, and the Cloth Hall, which are essential landmarks in the area.
